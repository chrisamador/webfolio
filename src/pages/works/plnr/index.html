"Just what the world needed, another calender app."

- Said No One

So why make another one?

I wanted to solve a missing feature all of the calendar apps I have use (it's been many) have.

Current calendar apps do a great job of placing boxes on your screen that designate your day's activities but none seem to know how those boxes connect to each other. That is where PLNR (pronounced planner) comes in.

What is PLNR?

PLNR is a side project I designed and development to practice functional front end programing. Using UX, UI, and modern JS techiques I created a working prototype.

Who's it for?

PLNR is meant for those who see their time as a limited resource, one that needs to be allocated and managed.
